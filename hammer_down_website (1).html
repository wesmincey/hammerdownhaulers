About Us

Family owned and operated. We value your business and trust. It is our goal to leave you with a clean and safe environment when the job is complete. We treat your property like it’s our own, and ensure your valuables are moved and delivered safely and on time.

My Story

I always dreamed of owning a business close to home. After college, I became a welder and joined Local Union 150, completing a 5-year apprenticeship. I worked as a journeyman welder for 5 years and spent the last 10+ years as a mechanical supervisor in nuclear power, paper mills, and chemical plants. Now, I’m proud to operate Hammer Down Haulers and make sure every customer is completely satisfied!
